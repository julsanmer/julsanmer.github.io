Dr. Julio C. Sanchez is a MSCA postdoctoral fellow at Universidad de Sevilla. He is developing his own body of research in machine-learning based GNC for autonomous small body exploration. Currently, his on-going project THOR focuses on autonomous gravity estimation in order to enable safer flight operations around asteroids. In the past, he has done research in the fields of spacecraft rendezvous and space surveillance.
